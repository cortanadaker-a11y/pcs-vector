## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Lead with NC teaching reciprocity: submit the licensure packet the day orders are received—expect 4–8 weeks. Register immediately for Cumberland County Schools substitute teaching (daily rate ~$120–150) to generate income while the full license processes. Use **MSEP and MyCAA** to cover any reciprocity fees or required coursework; schedule an ACS spouse employment workshop at the Soldier Support Center within the first week on station.

**Childcare bottleneck:** CDC waitlists run 30–90 days for school-age slots. Submit DD Form 2606 the same day orders drop and simultaneously contact Family Child Care homes in Hope Mills/Spring Lake (2–4 week openings typical). If both children need after-school care, budget for a private provider in the $600–800 range until a slot opens.
