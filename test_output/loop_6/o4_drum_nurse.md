**Primary recommendation: Lock on-post housing immediately upon orders and execute full DITY.** This sequence directly serves your cost-minimization priority while enabling fastest resettlement by eliminating rent exposure and leveraging the $5,114 DITY net to offset the $6,600 spouse income gap. On-post absorbs the full $2,655 BAH, keeps you under the $1,500 cap with zero out-of-pocket housing, and removes winter heating risk.
