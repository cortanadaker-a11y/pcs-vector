# PCS Vector Strategic Plan

## 1. Executive Summary & Recommended Strategy
**Primary recommendation: Prioritize off-post rental in Hope Mills (28348) or Spring Lake (28390) to secure spouse employment first.** This location gives the shortest path to Cumberland County Schools interviews and FCC childcare while staying inside your $1,800 cap. The $2,094 BAH (effective 2026-01-01) leaves a $644 surplus at the low end of rent, preserving cash flow for the 6–10 week spouse income gap.  

Ranked alternatives: (1) Raeford (28376) if school ratings outweigh commute, (2) on-post only as a 30-day bridge if off-post inventory collapses.  

**If NC licensure packet is delayed past day 21, fall back to substitute teaching via Cumberland County while completing reciprocity.**  

**Blind-spot insight:** All-American Fwy gate rush (0630–0800) will kill morning interviews; schedule everything after 0900 or you lose the first hiring window.  

**Soldier's Bottom Line:** Submit the NC teaching reciprocity packet and DD Form 2606 for CDC/FCC this week; delay past Friday and you risk both the school-year start and the $7,200 income gap widening.

## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Lead with NC licensure reciprocity (4–8 weeks typical). Submit the lateral-entry packet to Cumberland County Schools before arrival; packets received May–July get interview priority. Use MyCAA for any certification costs and register with MSEP and Hiring Our Heroes immediately.  

Fast-track options: Apply as a substitute while full license processes; ACS spouse employment workshops at the Soldier Support Center can accelerate this.  

**Dollar impact of a 4-week delay:** At the midpoint of the 6–10 week gap, you lose roughly $2,880 in spouse income on top of the already-estimated $7,200 total gap.  

Childcare bottleneck is real—CDC waitlists run 30–90 days for school-age. Submit DD Form 2606 the day orders drop and check ACS for current FCC openings in Hope Mills/Spring Lake (often 2–4 weeks). Military spouse programs (MSEP, MyCAA) can offset some certification fees but do not shorten the license timeline.
