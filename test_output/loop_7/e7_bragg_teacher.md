## 1. Executive Summary & Recommended Strategy
**Primary recommendation: Secure an off-post lease in Hope Mills (28348) or Spring Lake (28390) immediately upon arrival, execute a partial DITY, and fast-track spouse licensure through Cumberland County Schools before the school year starts.** This aligns with your top priority of spouse career continuity while delivering strong school zones and a $644 BAH surplus at the low end of market rents. ## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Lead with NC teaching license reciprocity (4–8 weeks typical) by submitting the lateral-entry packet to Cumberland County Schools before you clear Fort Hood. Cumberland hiring peaks May–July, so early submission gives interview priority. Use MyCAA to cover licensure costs and register for MSEP and Hiring Our Heroes workshops at the Soldier Support Center upon arrival. Family Child Care homes in Hope Mills/Spring Lake often open in 2–4 weeks versus 30–90 days at CDC—submit DD Form 2606 the day orders drop.

A 4-week licensure delay alone costs $3,600 in lost spouse income. Plan for 6–10 weeks to first paycheck; the $7,200 midpoint gap is the single largest cash-flow threat.
