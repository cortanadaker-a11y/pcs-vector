# PCS Vector Strategic Plan

## 1. Executive Summary & Recommended Strategy
For Catherine Webb, an O-3 Captain executing a Fort Bliss to Joint Base Lewis-McChord PCS in the 3–6 month window with a nursing spouse and two young children, the primary recommendation is to prioritize off-post housing in DuPont (98327) at the low end of market rent to lock in the full $736 monthly BAH surplus, run a full DITY for the projected $11,678 net, and sequence the spouse’s WA nursing endorsement from the losing station so income restarts inside six weeks. This path directly serves the family’s core goal of minimizing total costs while keeping the secondary priority of quick spouse employment on track. Ranked alternatives are Yelm for deeper rent savings if Madigan NAF becomes the target employer, or on-post if summer waitlists clear early and the family wants to eliminate rain-commute risk. If DuPont inventory is gone by arrival, fall back to Lakewood (98439) with a firm 20-minute commute cap rather than stretching to Yelm. The single biggest risk or dependency that collapses the plan if ignored is a delayed WA nursing endorsement creating a six-to-eight-week income gap in a high-rent market.

## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Housing and licensure decisions are inseparable for this family because a lease too far from Madigan can erase weeks of nursing income when rain and traffic hit. The fastest path starts with submitting the WA endorsement packet from Fort Bliss in week 0–1, applying simultaneously to Madigan NAF and MultiCare through MSEP and ACS Spouse Employment programs, then pursuing a temporary permit in weeks 2–3 so the first NAF paycheck lands by week 4–6 once childcare is secured. A four-week licensure slip alone costs roughly $4,400 in lost wages, and the 4–8 week window to first paycheck is realistic only if the family refuses to treat housing and job search as sequential steps. The critical bottleneck is reliable childcare—CDC waitlists run 30–60 days for preschoolers during PCS season—so the spouse cannot reliably interview or accept shifts until a lease is signed near the chosen employer.
