# PCS Vector Strategic Plan

## 1. Executive Summary & Recommended Strategy
**Primary recommendation: Secure an off-post rental in Hope Mills (28348) or Spring Lake (28390) within the first 10 days of arrival.** This directly supports the primary priority of spouse career momentum by placing the family in zones with faster Cumberland County Schools hiring cycles and shorter commutes to Cape Fear Valley Health and district offices. It also delivers a projected $400–$600 monthly BAH surplus that can offset licensure costs and summer utilities.

Ranked alternatives: (1) Raeford (28376) if inventory in Hope Mills dries up; (2) on-post only as a 30-day bridge if no suitable fenced rental is found by day 15.

The biggest risk here is the NC teaching license timeline slipping past the school-year start. **If licensure processing exceeds 6 weeks, fall back to substitute teaching via Cumberland County while completing the full credential packet.**

Blind-spot insight: Two-vehicle transport plus the 65-lb dog on a 1,180-mile haul increases the chance of damage claims or pet-stress delays; partial DITY keeps family logistics simpler while still netting meaningful payout.

## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Lead with NC licensure reciprocity. Submit the initial packet to the NC Department of Public Instruction the day orders are received; typical processing is 4–8 weeks. Use **MyCAA** to cover up to $4,000 in certification fees and **MSEP** for direct district connections at Cumberland County Schools.

Fast-track options: Apply immediately for substitute teaching (background check usually clears in 10–14 days) and lateral-entry provisional license while the full credential processes. Cumberland County and Cape Fear Valley Health both run rolling hires; target applications 45–60 days before the move window.

Childcare bottleneck: Fort Bragg CDC waitlists run 30–90 days for school-age slots. Submit DD Form 2606 the same day orders drop, then secure a Family Child Care (FCC) home in Hope Mills or Spring Lake as a 2–4 week bridge. This sequencing protects the spouse’s ability to start work before the school year begins.
