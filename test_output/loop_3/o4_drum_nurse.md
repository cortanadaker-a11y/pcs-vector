# PCS Vector Strategic Plan

## 1. Executive Summary & Recommended Strategy
**Primary recommendation: Secure on-post housing at Fort Drum while executing a full DITY/PPM.** This directly supports your priority of minimizing total costs by eliminating rent and leveraging BAH absorption, while the full DITY generates $1,500–4,000 net to offset winter heating and first-month expenses. Fastest resettlement follows by submitting the housing application and DD 2606 immediately upon orders.  

Key reasoning: On-post removes the $1,300–1,700 rent variable and snow-maintenance burden in a high-cost winter market; full DITY maximizes payout on the 980-mile move without conflicting with cost control.  

**If on-post waitlist exceeds 60 days, fall back to Evans Mills (13637) off-post at the low end of rent range.**  

Blind-spot insight: Even with on-post assignment, your toddler’s CDC slot could still force 3–6 months of higher-cost civilian childcare, eroding the cost savings you are prioritizing.

## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Submit nursing endorsement paperwork to the NY State Board within 10 days of arrival for the 4–8 week standard timeline; request the temporary permit (often granted in 2–3 weeks) to start at Samaritan Medical Center or on-post NAF positions immediately. Use **ACS Spouse Employment** and **Military Spouse Preference** for NAF hiring priority and **MyCAA** to cover any required CEUs at no cost.  

Realistic first paycheck: 4–6 weeks if temporary permit is approved. Bottleneck is the 3–6 month CDC waitlist—submit DD 2606 before departure and parallel-track FCC homes in Evans Mills/Le Ray for 2–4 week openings. Remote nursing options reduce winter travel risk while licensure processes.

## 3. Housing Strategy & Cost Tradeoffs
**Prioritize on-post assignment** to stay at $0 out-of-pocket rent and meet your $1,500 monthly cap while eliminating winter utility exposure. Off-post inventory moves fastest in Evans Mills; leverage your O-4 status and early application for negotiation room. Verify all BAH figures with finance before locking any lease.

## 4. Financial Opportunities & DITY/PPM Considerations
Execute **full DITY/PPM** on the 980-mile (2 driving days) move. Estimated government weight-allowance payout minus truck, fuel, and packing typically nets $1,500–4,000 when maximizing your O-4 allowance—higher than partial DITY. Use the payout to cover TLE (up to 10 days at new duty station) and first winter utility spike. Time the move to arrive mid-month so TLE bridges any gap before first BAH deposit.
