## 2. Spouse Career & Childcare Plan
Your spouse cannot wait for full NC licensure before earning income—the winning sequence is substitute pool plus lateral-entry application in parallel, not serial applications after arrival. Submit the NC DPI reciprocity packet and Cumberland County lateral-entry application from Fort Campbell in week 0–1 while registering for MyCAA reimbursement; complete fingerprinting and background in week 2–3 so substitute clearance lands in 10–14 days; begin paid substitute days ($120–150/day) in week 4–6 while the full contract processes; and target the first full paycheck in week 6–10 if the May–July hiring window is hit. Leverage MSEP for Cumberland County HR priority, MyCAA for up to $4,000 in certification costs, and ACS Employment Readiness workshops at the Soldier Support Center. The fastest paycheck path is the substitute pool, but it requires reliable childcare coverage first—CDC waitlists run 30–90 days for school-age slots, so submit DD Form 2606 the day orders drop and secure an FCC home in Hope Mills or Spring Lake as the immediate bridge. A four-week licensure delay alone costs roughly $3,600 in lost bridge income.
